PROJECTS

Welcome to my project gallery — a collection of what I’ve built, explored, and created. From hands-on coding challenges to creative media and research work, each project showcases the skills I’m developing and the interests I’m passionate about. Dive in to see what I’ve been working on!

C.A. Carlin

During my summer internship with C.A. Carlin, I got the opportunity to put some of my skills to the test in a real-world example. A highlight of my experience was presenting two projects: one offering a broad look at how AI is transforming business, and another focused specifically on sentiment analysis. For this, I analyzed TikTok comment data to uncover consumer emotions and trends, showing how CPG brands—especially in the candy category—can use these insights to better connect with customers in real time.

Machine Learning

Recently, I have been exploring advanced machine learning techniques, specifically Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Multi-Layer Perceptron Networks (MLPs). In this project, my goal is to develop a model capable of accurately classifying images from a dataset containing dogs, ships, cats, birds, and frogs. Through this process, I am working to refine my skills in image recognition, neural network architecture, and data-driven decision-making.

M.A.C Promotion

This flyer was part of a project completed for the Master of Arts program at Drake University. It is one of a series of three that I designed, each tailored to highlight different concentrations of the program. The project allowed me to develop skills in layout design, visual hierarchy, and messaging strategy while ensuring the materials aligned with the university’s branding guidelines. Together, the three flyers formed a cohesive set intended to engage prospective students and communicate the program’s unique value.
